Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak Korean to visit?

No. Dr. Kim Chaeyeon provides direct English consultation. Just mention "English" when booking.

Can foreigners use Korean health insurance?

If you are enrolled in Korean National Health Insurance (건강보험), all covered treatments apply equally. Without insurance, treatments are available at non-insured rates.

Is the clinic accessible by public transport?

Yes. We are a 5-minute walk from both Mapo Station (Line 6) and Gongdeok Station (Lines 5, 6, Airport Railroad, Gyeongui-Jungang Line).
